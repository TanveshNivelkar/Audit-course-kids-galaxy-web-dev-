ITALIAN IN SPIRIT

Welcome to THE CAL ZONE, the celebrated Italian oasis with two locations in the heart of downtown Milan, and international outposts in , Portugal and Dubai, U.A.E. THE CAL ZONE family includes a lively Italian style Diner and Juice Bar and a Italian Rum Bar & Jerk Shack. The Italian connection runs deep at THE CAL ZONE through its modern approach to classic Italia cooking, friendly and cool-as-can-be staff, and vibrant and colorful atmosphere.Our Italian menu is inspired by trips to Italy and created in collaboration with expert friend.You will find lots of choice including classic Italian food alongside our own signature take on a favourite dish or ingredient.

OUR RESTAURANTS

Escape the daily grind and step into our Italian-inspired interiors. We will start your meal with a lemonade taster as our way of saying make yourself at home. We have brought the al fresco indoors and added a touch of sparkle with disco balls and gold. Many of our restaurants feature bespoke artwork created to reflect the spirit of the community and drawing on the beauty of an Italian counterpart. Every one different but all with the same warm Italian welcome so you can relax and enjoy your time spent together around the table, just like the Italians do.
